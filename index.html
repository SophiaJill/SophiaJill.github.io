Where to start?

It's not just a thing of the past; archery is a wonderful hobby that takes time and commitment. A good bow will last a long time. Usually, archers will only need to change their draw weight as they gain experience.

A bow is nothing without arrows...

It all depends on the type of bow. Recurve or compound? From there, you can select arrows best suited for the type of bow. Feathered arrows are best suited for recurve bows, but finding a good brand for feathered arrows can be tricky.

Just some tidbits...

What do you want to do with archery? Compound bows are best suited for hunting, whereas recurve bows are best suited for target practice. However, they can be used either way depending on the archer's preference. Learning how to insert arrow tips or how to fletch your own arrows is a valuable skill to have; you can depend on your own work!
